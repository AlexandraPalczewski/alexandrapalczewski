Creating a Concept for an Open World Video Game

Rather than writing an end term essay for one of my master’s classes, I got permission to develop a concept for an open world video game because I had an idea that just wouldn’t go away, and I though it would be a more hands-on approach to showing what I learned through the course. This was also my first attempt at concept art using digital painting techniques in Photoshop. The manual below is a walkthrough of the story line, and through it I hope to give the reader a sense of the gameplay.
