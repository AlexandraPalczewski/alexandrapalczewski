My name's Alex!

I'm a UX designer, illustrator, and recent graduate of the University of Waterloo's XDM Master's program. Currently based in Waterloo, I've been (researching the intersection between visual design and).

Previously at Descartes Systems Group, I was applying color theory to redesign a software's UI to be color-blind accessible and meet WCAG standards. I also developed another application's button hierarchy and redesigned their navigation bar to improve user flow.

At Transformative Quantum Technologies, I worked with quantum researchers to make illustrations of their work to present to the public. During my time there, I also created a graphic illustration to showcase quantum funding to the Prime Minister's Cabinet. And in my free time, I make huge abstract paintings, practice skateboard tricks, and create concepts for videogames.
